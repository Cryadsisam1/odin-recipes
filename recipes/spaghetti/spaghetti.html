Spaghetti recipe

Home

This spaghetti carbonara is a super rich "bacon and egg" pasta dish that's great to serve for company.

This recipe also makes an unusual brunch offering.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil, divided, or as needed
  • 8 slices of bacon, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of dry white wine
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped, fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese

How to prepare spaghetti?

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted wate to boil, then cook spaghetti for 12 minutes and drain it. Put then 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  2. Cook diced bacon for 10 minutes, or until evenly browned. Drain then bacon on paper towels, remembering to save 2 tablespoons of bacon fat.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil to bacon fat, add chopped onion and cook on medium head until onion is translucent. Add minced garlic and wine and cook for 1 more minute.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti. Add olive oil if it seems dry. Add then beaten eggs, and cook until eggs are barely set. Quickly add 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve warm with chopped parsley sprinked on top and extra parmesan cheese.
