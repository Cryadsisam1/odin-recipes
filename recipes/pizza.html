Pizza on the grill is a fantastic way to make pizza at home. The head of a hot grill is a prefect match for a professional pizza oven

Learn the tricks for making and gilling pizza from scratch with this recipe. Feel free to use your favorite toppings, but keep in mind not to overload the pizza.

What are the required ingredients?

Pizza dough:
  • 1 cup of warm water (45 degrees Celsius)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package of active dry yeast
  • 1 pinch of white sugar
  • 3 1/3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons of kosher salt
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh basil
Garlic oil:
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
Pizza toppings:
  • 1/4 cup of tomato sauce, divided
  • 1 cup of chopped tomatoes, divided
  • 1/4 cup of sliced black olives, divided
  • 1/4 cup of roasted red peppers, drained and chopped, divided
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 4 tablespoons of chopped fresh basil, divided

How to prepare pizza?

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Make the garlic oil, then brush it onto the grill grates.
  3. Shape the dough and place it on the hot grill.
  4. Flip the crust and brush it with oil.
  5. Top the pizza however you like, close the lid, and cook until the cheese melts.
